About

Hi! My name is Baruch Geuze, I create AR experiences for brands and personalities.

Started in 2018 as a hobby, Social media AR became my job quickly during 2021. Thanks to software like Effect House, Lens Studio, Spark AR Studio and Blender, right now I'm able to transform almost any idea into augmented reality.
